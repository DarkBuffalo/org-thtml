#+title: Strawberry smoothie
#+date: <2020-04-22 mi.>

* Ingredients
- 250ml almond milk
- 3 table spoons of oats
- 100g of ripe strawberries, washed and clean from leaves.

* Steps
1. Blend the mixture with an immersion blender.
2. Enjoy :-)
